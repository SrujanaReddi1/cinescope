About the Movie

Kishkindapuri is a suspense-filled horror thriller starring Bellamkonda Sreenivas and Anupama Parameswaran. The story follows a fake ghost-hunting team that accidentally awakens a dangerous supernatural force after entering an abandoned radio station.
